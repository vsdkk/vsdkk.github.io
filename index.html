About Me

My name is Vlad [vsdkk]. I’ve been actively working in the blockchain space since 2021. I started with investing in ICOs, IDOs, launchpads, and new projects out of curiosity. Over time, it became more than just an interest: I began diving deeper into running nodes, testnets, and the internal processes of projects to understand how everything actually works in practice.

Since then, I’ve worked across different ecosystems DeFi, AI, and Layer 1/2 protocols. I’ve spent a lot of time in communities and ambassador programs, where real user interaction happens. Today, I’m a full-time contributor, and I enjoy working with people: communication, moderation, engagement, support, and creating clear and understandable context.
